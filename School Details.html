School Details

Name: Wilson's School

Address:Mollison Drive, Wallington

Principal:Nathan Cole

Grades: Year 7 to Year 13

Enrollment: Approximately 1300 students

Streams Offered:Broad curriculum including English, maths, science (separated), history, geography, French or German, Latin, art, computing, design technology (DT), cookery, drama, music, PE, and PSHE.

Educational Board:It is not under a specific commercial educational board. It is a state-funded selective boys' grammar school in Wallington, Sutton, that follows the national curriculum and uses a two-stage entrance exam, including the Sutton Selective Eligibility Test (SET), for admissions.
